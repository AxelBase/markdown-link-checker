Markdown Link Checker vs. Online Validators

Published: September 26, 2025 | Last Updated: September 26, 2025

Online validators often require uploads, log your data, and offer limited transparency. Our Markdown Link Checker runs locally, respects your privacy, and gives you full control.

Key Differences

  • Client-side only: No uploads, no tracking
  • Configurable: Timeout, ignore patterns, concurrency
  • Exportable: TXT and JSON formats
  • Transparent: Open-source and auditable

Why It Matters

Privacy-conscious users need tools that don’t compromise autonomy. Our validator is built for skeptics, educators, and compliance teams.

FAQ

Do online validators store my content?

Many do. Ours doesn’t—everything runs in your browser.

Is this tool open-source?

Yes. You can audit or fork it on GitHub.

Can I use both tools?

Sure—but for sensitive content, client-side is safer.
