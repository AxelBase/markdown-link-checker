Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Markdown Link Checker. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our service. By accessing or using the tool, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Information We Collect
We collect data you provide during use, including Markdown files or text you upload or paste for link validation. This may include URLs, text content, and file names. We do not collect personal information such as names or email addresses unless you voluntarily submit them via the contact form. Technical data, like IP addresses and browser types, may be logged to improve service performance and troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Your Data
Your uploaded Markdown content is processed solely to validate links and generate results, which are displayed or exported as per your request. We use technical data to monitor system stability and enhance functionality. No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Contact form submissions are used only to respond to your inquiries.

Data Security
We implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Uploaded content is processed temporarily and deleted from our servers after validation unless retained for debugging with your consent. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not share your data with external parties except as required by law or to protect our rights. In such cases, we will notify you unless prohibited. Service providers assisting with hosting or analytics may access aggregated, anonymized data, but they are bound by confidentiality agreements.

Your Choices
You control the data you submit. Avoid including sensitive information in Markdown files or contact forms. To request data deletion or raise concerns, contact us via the form on this site. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. The latest version will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

For questions, contact us through the site’s contact form. Your trust is our priority.
